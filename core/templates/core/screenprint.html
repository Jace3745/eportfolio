Screenprinting Project

This is my second webpage for this course.

Thinking of ideas

In my new media and technology class, my teacher assigned me a screen printing project. First are teacher gave us some inspiration like examples of Uncle ben some other inspirational pictures for inspiration. A lot of inspirational ones spoke to me so I dedcided to make one to make people feel better. I dedcided to do a thumbs up and a message like It's ok it get better.

Taking pictures

The next step was starting to take photos to use in photoshop. With the help of my tablemates, they helped me take picture to use. I also made sure to help them take pictures.The picture went with had a blank backround with my right hand giving a thumbs up.

Photoshop

Next I downloaded my photo and put it in to phototshop. There a made layers and edits to my picture.The most important thing to do was to make sure that I had only one or 2 colors. I only used one color which was like a light orange brown color.

Illustrator

After finishing in photoshop I moved it into illustrator to make mt cut list and my color mixing guide. This also allowed me to know what I have to do when I get to later steps.

Cutting the wood

Now it was time to cut my wood we used furring strips. My teacher brought out the saws for us to start cutting I cut my wood according to the cut list in total I cut it in to 4 parts.

Stapling and Glueing

Aftering sawing it was time to glue and staple. I started by putting the glue in between my pieces of wood.Followed by stapling it to keep it in place.

Cutting and Stretching

Now it is time to cut my screen to stretch on my frame. I cut the screen according to my cut list.Cutting my screen took a couple trys but I got it perfect. Now I stapled and stretched my screen onto my frame with help from my teacher and my tablemates.Then I tapped over the staples.

Vinyl Cutting and Heat Pressing

Now use my photoshop png file to start cutting my vinyl. I cut out a piece of vinyl according to my cut list. I uploaded my png to silhouette studio so I can upload to png to the vinyl cutting machine. After ti finished cutting I heat pressed the vinyl into my screen make sure to get the corners. To finish i tapped the inbetween the frame to make sure paint doesn't go through where I dont want it to.

Mixing the Paint and pushing paint through screen

Now I started to mix my paint to make my desired color. I used yellow red white and a drop of black to get my color to look like the one on my cut list. After I got my color it was now time push the paint throught the screen. After that was done had my final product and I loved it.
