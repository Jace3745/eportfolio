Black Box investgation

Introduction

In our New Media and Tech class we were assigned to investigate a Black box. We made another box on what we thought was in this black box.When you pour water into the box it either outputs more,less, or the same amount of water. This box had few intresting qualites which made this project hard.

Claim

We believe that there are 2 containers with a bucket that goes into a draining system.

Evidence

The water took a while to come out and sometimes didn't come out at all. It made a dripping sound when pouring water through a funnel. Also sometimes more water came out then put in. This also happened the other way around meaning less water came out then put in. Are model is similar. Are model had more water drip out then put in but didnt output less water then put in only the same or less. It has the dripping sound from the normal box. SO I think are model was a good match. Also water did spill so more could have came out.

Reasoning, Part I

Our model uses the bucket to hold extra water to come out and also water not to come out at all.

Reasoning, Part II

The dripping water sound was water dripping into bucket this explains why we heard a sound when pouring the water because of the drop
